# 🍱 FoodClose — Save Food. Save Money.

A sleek, fast web app that helps people discover **surplus food at discounted prices** — reducing waste while making good food more affordable.

---

## 🌍 What is FoodClose?

FoodClose connects users with nearby surplus food deals from vendors. Instead of letting food go to waste, it gives it a second chance — at a lower price.

---

## 🧠 Why this project?

Food waste is a huge problem. This project explores a simple idea:

> What if leftover food could be easily sold instead of thrown away?

FoodClose is a small step toward that.
